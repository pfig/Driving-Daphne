My entry for the OraMIX competition, using the stems provided by the Science Museum and synthetic noise.

My idea for this was the thought of satellites unintelligible to (and oblivious of) us, talking continuously to each other, transmitting the most inordinate amount of mostly useless information (fact: 83.2% of satellite traffic are TV broadcasts).
